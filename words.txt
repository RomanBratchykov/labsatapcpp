Tim sat on a fallen log, watching the sun dip low. A small dog ran past, ears up. Tim saw him stop and hide behind a bush. He looked scared. Tim stood up and walked over. The dog had a red tag. Ben was on it. Tim looked around but saw no one. The dog wagged his tail. Tim patted his head. They walked to town. A girl ran up and held the dog tight. She smiled and looked happy.
